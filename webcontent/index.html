About Tembaro

Tembaro is a beautiful zone located in the Southern Nations, Nationalities, and Peoples' Region of Ethiopia. Known for its stunning landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality, Tembaro offers visitors an authentic Ethiopian experience.
